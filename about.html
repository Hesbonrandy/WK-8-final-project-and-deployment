Welcome to MonRan

Your Second Place, After Home and Office

Where Community Brews

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Kinoo by Monica and Randy, two Nairobi-based remote workers who missed the buzz of human connection, Kinoo was born to be your “third place” not home, not work, but somewhere in between. Where the coffee’s hot, the Wi-Fi’s free, and the neighbors become friends.

“Why can’t work feel like home but better?”

What started as a dream over mandazi and Kenyan filter coffee is now a vibrant hub where freelancers, students, creatives, and neighbors gather not just for coffee, but for conversation, collaboration, and belonging.

We are more than a café. We’re your “third space” where deadlines meet laughter, Wi-Fi is free, and strangers become friends over a perfectly brewed cup.

Our Mission

“To create warm, inclusive spaces; one cup at a time.”

Angela Monica & Hesbon Randy

Monica:National Barista Champion 2024. Grew up helping her mum roast coffee in Mombasa. Believes every cup should start a conversation.

Randy:Former UX designer for a Nairobi fintech startup. Missed the watercooler chats and whiteboard sessions. Believes good design includes human warmth.

Together, they transformed a dusty old shop on Waiyaki Way into Nairobi’s coziest coworking café complete with Afro-jazz playlists, charging ports, and the best view of the jacaranda trees.
